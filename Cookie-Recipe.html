Bst Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is frommy dad and they are a favorite among friends and family. The secret ingredient is the coconout! Be Warned, these will fly off the plate!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanila
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconout

Directions

  1. Preheat the overn to 325
  2. BEat the butter, sugar, eggs, and Vanilla together until creamy
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl
  4. Add flour to the mixture slowly
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconout
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
